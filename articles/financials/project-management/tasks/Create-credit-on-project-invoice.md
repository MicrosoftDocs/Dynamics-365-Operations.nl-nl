1. <span data-ttu-id="0ac48-104">Ga naar Projectbeheer en boekhouding > Projecten > Alle projecten.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0ac48-104">Go to Project management and accounting > Projects > All projects.</span></span>
