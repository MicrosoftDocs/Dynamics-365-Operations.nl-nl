[!include [task guide banner](../../includes/task-guide-banner.md)]

<span data-ttu-id="c08b1-104">In deze procedure ziet hoe u een vast activum afstoot met een vrije-tekstfactuur.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c08b1-104">This procedure shows how to dispose of a fixed asset using the free text invoice.</span></span>

1. <span data-ttu-id="c08b1-105">Ga naar Klanten > Facturen > Alle vrije-tekstfacturen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c08b1-105">Go to Accounts receivable > Invoices > All free text invoices.</span></span>
2. <span data-ttu-id="c08b1-106">Klik op Nieuw.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c08b1-106">Click New.</span></span>
3. <span data-ttu-id="c08b1-107">Typ of selecteer een waarde in het veld Klantrekening.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c08b1-107">In the Customer account field, enter or select a value.</span></span>

6. <span data-ttu-id="c08b1-110">Voer een omschrijving in op de factuurregel.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c08b1-110">Enter a Description into the invoice line.</span></span>

10. <span data-ttu-id="c08b1-114">Klik op Regeldetails.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c08b1-114">Click Line details.</span></span>  
11. <span data-ttu-id="c08b1-115">Selecteer het vaste-activanummer voor verkoop.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c08b1-115">Select the Fixed asset number to be sold.</span></span>
12. <span data-ttu-id="c08b1-116">Klik op Boeken.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c08b1-116">Click Post.</span></span>
