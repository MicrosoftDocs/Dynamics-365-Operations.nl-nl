[!include [task guide banner](../../includes/task-guide-banner.md)]

## <a name="validate-year-end-close-parameters"></a><span data-ttu-id="412d6-105">Parameters voor de jaarafsluiting valideren</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="412d6-105">Validate year-end close parameters</span></span>

2. <span data-ttu-id="412d6-107">Vouw de sectie Afsluiting boekjaar uit.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="412d6-107">Expand the Fiscal year close section.</span></span>
