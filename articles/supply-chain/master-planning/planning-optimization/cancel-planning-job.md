# <a name="cancel-a-planning-job"></a><span data-ttu-id="4fe17-103">Een planningstaak annuleren</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="4fe17-103">Cancel a planning job</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="4fe17-104">In Microsoft Dynamics 365 Supply Chain Management kunt u een actieve planningstaak annuleren die gebruikmaakt van de functionaliteit Optimalisatie van planning.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="4fe17-104">In Microsoft Dynamics 365 Supply Chain Management, you can cancel an active planning job that uses the Planning Optimization functionality.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="4fe17-105">Voer de volgende stappen uit om een actieve planningstaak te annuleren.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="4fe17-105">To cancel an active planning job, follow these steps.</span></span>

> [!NOTE]
> <span data-ttu-id="4fe17-106">Alleen actieve taken kunnen worden geannuleerd.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="4fe17-106">Only active jobs can be canceled.</span></span>

1. <span data-ttu-id="4fe17-107">Ga naar **Hoofdplanning \> Instellingen \> Plannen**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="4fe17-107">Go to **Master planning \> Setup \> Plans**.</span></span>
2. <span data-ttu-id="4fe17-108">Selecteer een geschikt plan voor de planningsuitvoering.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="4fe17-108">Select an appropriate plan for the planning run.</span></span>
3. <span data-ttu-id="4fe17-109">Selecteer **Historie**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="4fe17-109">Select **History**.</span></span>
4. <span data-ttu-id="4fe17-110">Selecteer de planningstaak die u wilt annuleren</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="4fe17-110">Select the planning job to cancel.</span></span>
5. <span data-ttu-id="4fe17-111">Selecteer **Annuleren**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="4fe17-111">Select **Cancel**.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="4fe17-112">De taakstatus is **Annuleren** totdat de service Optimalisatie van planning bevestigt dat de taak is geannuleerd.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="4fe17-112">The job status will be **Canceling** until the Planning Optimization service confirms that the job has been canceled.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="4fe17-113">De status wordt vervolgens gewijzigd in **Geannuleerd**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="4fe17-113">The status will then be changed to **Canceled**.</span></span>
