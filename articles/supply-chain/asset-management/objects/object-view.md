    > [!NOTE]

<span data-ttu-id="64345-112">Aan de rechterkant van de pagina bieden sneltabbladen details van de geselecteerde weergave.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="64345-112">On the right side of the page, FastTabs shows details of the selected view.</span></span>
