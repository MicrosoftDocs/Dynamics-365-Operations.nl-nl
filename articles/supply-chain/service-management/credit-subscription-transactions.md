## <a name="credit-subscription-transactions"></a><span data-ttu-id="a6a60-104">Abonnementstransacties crediteren</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a6a60-104">Credit subscription transactions</span></span>

1.  <span data-ttu-id="a6a60-105">Klik op **Servicebeheer** \> **Algemeen** \> **Serviceabonnementen** \> **Alle serviceabonnementen**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a6a60-105">Click **Service management** \> **Common** \> **Service subscriptions** \> **All service subscriptions**.</span></span>
