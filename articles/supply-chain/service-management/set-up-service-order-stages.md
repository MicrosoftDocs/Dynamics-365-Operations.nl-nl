1.  <span data-ttu-id="76b04-104">Klik op **Servicebeheer** \> **Instellen** \> **Serviceorders** \> **Servicefasen**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="76b04-104">Click **Service management** \> **Setup** \> **Service orders** \> **Service stages**.</span></span>
