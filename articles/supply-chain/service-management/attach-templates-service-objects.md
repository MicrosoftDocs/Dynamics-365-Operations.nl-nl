# <a name="attach-templates-to-service-objects"></a><span data-ttu-id="2f104-103">Sjablonen aan serviceobjecten koppelen</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2f104-103">Attach templates to service objects</span></span>    

[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]


<span data-ttu-id="2f104-104">U kunt een sjabloonstuklijst aan een serviceobjectrelatie koppelen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2f104-104">A template bill of materials (BOM) can be attached to a service object relation.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2f104-105">Vervolgens kunt u de serviceobjectrelatie aan serviceorders of serviceovereenkomsten koppelen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2f104-105">The service object relation can then be associated with either service orders or service agreements.</span></span>

1.  <span data-ttu-id="2f104-106">Klik op **Servicebeheer** \> **Instellen** \> **Serviceobjecten** \> **Serviceobjecten**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2f104-106">Click **Service management** \> **Setup** \> **Service objects** \> **Service objects**.</span></span>

2.  <span data-ttu-id="2f104-107">Selecteer een serviceobject.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2f104-107">Select a service object.</span></span>
