2. <span data-ttu-id="a7234-106">Selecteer **Nieuw**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a7234-106">Select **New**.</span></span>
3. <span data-ttu-id="a7234-107">Typ of selecteer een waarde in het veld **Klantrekening**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a7234-107">In the **Customer account** field, enter or select a value.</span></span>

11. <span data-ttu-id="a7234-115">Selecteer **Boeken**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a7234-115">Select **Post**.</span></span>
