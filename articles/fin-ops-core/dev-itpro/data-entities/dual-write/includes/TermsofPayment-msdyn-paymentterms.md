ISCASHPAYMENT | >< | msdyn_iscashpayment |

ISCERTIFIEDCOMPANYCHECK | >< | msdyn_iscertifiedcompanycheck |

CREDITCARDCREDITCHECKTYPE | >< | msdyn_creditcardcreditchecktype |
